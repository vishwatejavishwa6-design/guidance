National Education Policy 2020

The National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020) aims to transform India's education system to meet the demands of the 21st century. It focuses on holistic, flexible, and multidisciplinary education, moving away from rote learning to critical thinking and experiential learning.

Key Reforms:

  • Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) from age 3.
  • New 5+3+3+4 curricular structure.
  • No rigid separation between arts and sciences, curricular and extra-curricular activities, vocational and academic streams.
  • Emphasis on foundational literacy and numeracy.
  • Increased focus on vocational education and digital learning.
  • Establishment of a National Research Foundation.
