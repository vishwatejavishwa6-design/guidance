Interview Tips

  • Research the Company: Understand their mission, values, and recent projects.
  • Practice Common Questions: Prepare answers for behavioral and technical questions.
  • Dress Professionally: Make a good first impression.
  • Ask Thoughtful Questions: Shows your interest and engagement.
  • Send a Thank-You Note: Follow up after the interview to reiterate your interest.
  • Be Punctual: Arrive early, whether in-person or virtual.
  • Maintain Eye Contact: Shows confidence and engagement.
  • Be Honest & Confident: Present your skills and experience genuinely.
  • Show Enthusiasm: Let your passion for the role shine through.
  • Prepare Your Portfolio: If applicable, have your work ready to showcase.
