1. IBPS (Institute of Banking Personnel Selection) Exams

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) is an autonomous body in India that recruits young graduates for various public sector banks. IBPS conducts common recruitment processes (CRPs) for various posts like Probationary Officer (PO), Clerk, and Regional Rural Banks (RRB).

Exams Covered: IBPS PO/MT, IBPS Clerk, IBPS RRB Officer (Scale I, II, III) & Office Assistant

Subjects:

  • Quantitative Aptitude: Data Interpretation, Simplification, Number Series, Ratio & Proportion, Percentage, Averages, Time & Work, etc.
  • Reasoning Ability: Puzzles, Seating Arrangement, Syllogism, Blood Relations, Coding-Decoding, Inequalities, etc.
  • English Language: Reading Comprehension, Cloze Test, Spotting Errors, Para Jumbles, Sentence Improvement, etc.
  • General Awareness (with special reference to Banking Awareness): Current Affairs, Banking & Financial Awareness, Static GK.
  • Computer Aptitude: Fundamentals of Computer, Internet, MS Office, Networking, etc. (for specific exams)

Official Links: IBPS Official Website | IBPS PO | IBPS Clerk

2. SBI (State Bank of India) Exams

State Bank of India (SBI) is the largest public sector bank in India, known for its extensive network and diverse banking services. SBI conducts its own independent recruitment exams for various positions including Probationary Officer (PO) and Clerk (Junior Associate).

Exams Covered: SBI PO, SBI Clerk (Junior Associate)

Subjects:

  • Reasoning Ability: Logical Reasoning, Alphanumeric Series, Ranking, Direction Sense, Data Sufficiency, etc.
  • Quantitative Aptitude: Simplification & Approximation, Quadratic Equations, Data Interpretation, Profit & Loss, Simple & Compound Interest, etc.
  • English Language: Vocabulary, Grammar, Reading Comprehension, Sentence Rearrangement, Phrase Replacement, etc.
  • General Awareness / Financial Awareness: Current Affairs, Banking & Financial Terms, Economy News, Static GK related to India and Banking.

Official Links: SBI Careers Official Page | Current Vacancies

3. RBI (Reserve Bank of India) Exams

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) is India's central bank and regulatory body. RBI conducts highly sought-after examinations for positions like Grade B Officer and Assistant, offering prestigious career opportunities in economic policy and financial regulation.

Exams Covered: RBI Grade B Officer (General, DEPR, DSIM), RBI Assistant

Subjects (RBI Grade B - General):

  • Phase I: General Awareness, English Language, Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning Ability.
  • Phase II: Economic & Social Issues (ESI), English (Writing Skills), Finance & Management (FM).

Subjects (RBI Assistant):

  • Prelims: English Language, Numerical Ability, Reasoning Ability.
  • Mains: English Language, Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning Ability, Computer Knowledge, General Awareness.

Official Links: RBI Recruitment Page | RBI Grade B Information

4. NABARD Exams

The National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) is an apex development financial institution in India, focusing on rural development. NABARD recruits for Grade A and Grade B positions, targeting professionals passionate about agriculture and rural sectors.

Exams Covered: NABARD Grade A (Assistant Manager), NABARD Grade B (Manager)

Subjects (Grade A/B):

  • Phase I: Reasoning, English Language, Computer Knowledge, Quantitative Aptitude, General Awareness, Economic & Social Issues (ESI), Agriculture & Rural Development (ARD).
  • Phase II: ESI, ARD (Objective & Descriptive), General English (Descriptive).

Official Links: NABARD Careers | NABARD Grade A Details

5. Insurance Sector Exams (Government-owned)

The government-owned insurance companies offer stable and rewarding careers in the financial sector. Exams like LIC AAO, LIC ADO, and NIACL AO recruit for various administrative and development officer roles.

Exams Covered: LIC AAO (Assistant Administrative Officer), LIC ADO (Apprentice Development Officer), NIACL AO (Administrative Officer), GIC AO, OICL AO, UIIC AO

Subjects:

  • Reasoning Ability: Puzzles, Seating Arrangement, Syllogism, Input-Output, Data Sufficiency, etc.
  • Quantitative Aptitude: Data Interpretation, Simplification, Number Series, Ratio & Proportion, Percentage, Time & Distance, etc.
  • English Language: Reading Comprehension, Grammar, Vocabulary, Descriptive English.
  • General Awareness: Current Affairs, Static GK, Insurance & Financial Market Awareness.
  • Computer Knowledge: Basic computer operations, MS Office, Internet.
  • Insurance & Financial Market Awareness: History of Insurance, IRDAI, types of insurance, financial products.

Official Links: LIC Careers | NIACL Careers | GIC Re Careers
