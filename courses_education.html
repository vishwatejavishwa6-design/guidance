Courses & Educational Opportunities

Explore popular courses, essential entrance exams, and valuable scholarship opportunities to support your academic journey.

Popular Courses After 10th Grade

  • Science Stream: PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Math), PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), PCMB (Physics, Chemistry, Math, Biology)
  • Commerce Stream: Accounts, Business Studies, Economics, Math/Informatics Practices
  • Arts/Humanities Stream: History, Political Science, Economics, Sociology, Psychology, Literature
  • Vocational/Diploma Courses: Polytechnic, ITI, Paramedical, Hotel Management, etc.

Popular Courses After 12th Grade

  • Engineering: B.Tech/B.E. (various specializations)
  • Medicine: MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, B.Pharma
  • Commerce: B.Com, BBA, BMS, CA, CS
  • Arts/Humanities: B.A. (various subjects), Journalism, Mass Communication, BSW
  • Science: B.Sc. (various subjects), BCA, B.Arch
  • Law: BA.LL.B., BBA.LL.B. (integrated 5-year courses)

Key Entrance Exams

  • Engineering: JEE Main, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, MHT CET, VITEEE
  • Medical: NEET UG
  • Law: CLAT, LSAT India, AILET
  • Management: IPMAT (for Integrated BBA+MBA), CUET UG (for various central university courses)
  • Design: NID DAT, UCEED, NIFT Entrance Exam

Note: Specific exam details and application dates vary. Always refer to official websites for the latest information.

Main Scholarship Links

  • National Scholarship Portal (NSP): Government scholarships for various categories.
  • Buddy4Study: India's largest scholarship platform.
  • UGC Scholarships: University Grants Commission scholarships.
  • Sitaram Jindal Foundation Scholarship: For various courses.

Always verify scholarship eligibility and application deadlines from official sources.
